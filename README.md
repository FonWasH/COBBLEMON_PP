**_Did you like Cobbleverse? You will love Cobblemon++._**

**Cobblemon++** is based on **[Cobbleverse](https://modrinth.com/modpack/cobbleverse)**, but it is not just a simple update; it is a complete overhaul designed for players who want the best of both worlds: Pokémon catching and a technical, smooth, and community-oriented Minecraft experience. Whether you are a solo trainer or a guild leader, this modpack has been sculpted to offer total immersion without unnecessary distractions.

## Highlights

- **100% French experience:** the modpack is fully translated into French for smooth navigation and immediate understanding of all items and quests.
- **Offline-mode Ready:** play wherever you want, whenever you want with account protection and skin changes, even for servers in offline-mode.
- **Advanced Technology & Storage:** no more messy chests! With more elaborate technologies and machinery, automate your harvests and manage your items digitally. Science is at the service of your Pokéballs.
- **Exploration & Challenges:** the world is richer than ever. Discover new structures and take on new challenges to prove your worth as a trainer.
- **Multiplayer, better:** protect your bases. Teleport your friends. Dungeon chests are individual: each player receives their own loot, no more fighting over treasure!
- **Quality of Life (QoL):** several gameplay comforts have been added. Enjoy a borderless windowed mode for perfect visual comfort and compatibility with HDR screens. You can chop down entire trees without difficulty. And much more.
- **Cobblemon-Friendly Aesthetics:** we have cleaned the modpack of all "egotrip" content to provide a clean, consistent interface centered on the Pokémon and Cobblemon universe.
